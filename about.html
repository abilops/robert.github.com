About me

I work on security at Stripe. I'm interested in online tracking, being sneaky, stopping the internet from ruining my life, economics, teaching programming, reading, Magic: The Gathering, and writing stories. I also like chess, but I haven't written any blog posts about that. I used to live in San Francisco. Now I live in London.

I do weekly office hours where I talk about programming with anyone who signs up. I think they are most useful for "Advanced Beginners" who are working towards getting their first job as a programmer. But regardless of who you are, if this sounds interesting and useful then I'd love to hear from you.

I'm on Twitter and I use email.
